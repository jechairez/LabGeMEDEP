S2 Appendix. Logic Rules of FOS-GNR for Matlab coding
Description: The transformation of logic rules are based on the next:

In cases where x(t+1)=!A(t) , it is transformed into x(t+1)=MN A (MN is a matrix related to negation)
In cases where x(t+1)=A(t)&B(t) , it is transformed into x(t+1)= MC A B (MC is a matrix related to conjunction)
In cases where x(t+1)=A(t)|B(t) , it is transformed into x(t+1)= MD A B (MD is a matrix related to disjunction)
